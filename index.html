Drug addiction

The lion and the cheetah

Introduction

What is the story
The Lion is addicted to "red meat" and the cheetah attempts to stop the lion from his addiction. The lion is meant to represent addiction. Meaning something you always go to something you can't just stop easily. The way the lion keeps going for the red meat is actually indicating a addiction to drugs without actually showing drugs.
What was Used
Dragon frame - To create different lighings to creat different emotion/feeling to the scene. Clay - To create characters. (The lion and Cheetah) Box - Made for the creations to stand on. As well as to create backgroud
